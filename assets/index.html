setTimeout as a Service

Specify the amount of time (defaults to milliseconds) you want the server to sleep before responding.
Supports HTTP requests with the time being the path or socket connections on port 5103 with the time sent followed by a newline.
You can also send format modifiers like ?json, ?css, ?callback=test, ?js, ?int.

Examples

1000 would sleep for 1000 milliseconds

10s would sleep for 10 seconds

1h5s would sleep for 1 hour and 5 seconds

Source: http://github.com/fastest963/settimeout
